## üìñ Short Description

**Kawayanihan Web App** is a centralized digital platform designed to support the **Kawayanihan Circular Economy Movement**. It serves as a management and monitoring tool for bamboo planting initiatives, volunteer coordination, and resource inventory tracking.

By leveraging technology, this application aims to streamline the "bayanihan" (community spirit) efforts in environmental sustainability, connecting local farmers, government agencies, and volunteers to ensure the long-term success of reforestation and livelihood programs.

## ‚ú® Features

* **Interactive Dashboard**: Real-time visualization of planting statistics, survival rates, and community impact data.
* **Bamboo Resource Inventory**: Tools for logging and tracking bamboo growth stages, species, and harvest readiness.
* **Volunteer Management**: Registration, event scheduling, and task assignment for community planting drives.
* **Geo-Tagging & Mapping**: Integration with map services to pinpoint planting sites and monitor land coverage.
* **Reporting System**: Automated generation of progress reports for stakeholders and funding partners.
* **Marketplace Integration** (Future): Connecting bamboo farmers directly with buyers and processors.
